Adventures

From Beijing to Tokyo and Venice to Athens, traveling is one of my life's greatest pursuits. I love to adventure and have visited many countries, yet there are still countless more places and cultures to explore. One day, I hope to be able to truthfully claim having visited them all.

Food

Sushi, Foie Gras, Wagyu, Hainan Chicken, Indian Curry, Arabian Shawarma, In-N-Out, and more – I love it all. Food is another one of my great passions. Not only do I love to eat, but I love to cook as well, spending a fair portion of every other day experimenting in the kitchen.

Games

I am a Gamer

I love games for the challenges they pose, the stories they weave, and the creativity they represent. To me, videogames are the new popular medium for artistic innovation. From the cooperative adventures of Destiny to the masterfully crafted tale of Witcher, videogames offer an unparalleled opportunity to become the heroes (or villains) of one's dreams.
